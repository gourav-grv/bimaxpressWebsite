Mission 100

In today's world we want to bring everyone together and do our bit in breaking the Chain of COVID. With this vision we launch our Mission 1000. Through this mission we are going to support people in need to get access to medical essential items required for the fight against covid 19. You can also be part of this mission by donating an amount for this mission. Help us in raising funds and achieving our mission to help 1000 families in need. Let us break this chain together.

Every contribution matters !!

Covid Isolated?

Register yourself for kit

Are you a Home Isolated patient w RTPCR report not older than 5 days? If yes, then please register on Bimaxpress website to be eligible for a free CORONA Kit being delivered to you to your doorstep. Bimaxpress has undertaken MISSION 1000 to help 1000 Covid impacted individuals and families by providing CORONA Kits to them. CORONA Kit will comprise of Oxymeter, Vaporizer, Thermometer, Gloves, Masks, Sanitizer. Stay home & stay safe and lets break the chain together!
